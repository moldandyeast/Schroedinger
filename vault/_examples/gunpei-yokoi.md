# Lateral Thinking with Withered Technology

Gunpei Yokoi was an engineer at Nintendo. While other companies chased cutting-edge technology, Yokoi advocated for a different approach:

> Take mature, well-understood, cheap technology and use it in radical new ways.

This philosophy produced the Game Boy. Competitors built color handhelds with expensive screens and short battery life. Yokoi used a simple monochrome LCD from the calculator industry.
